WordPress website

WordPress is still my go-to Content Management System (CMS) simply because it's so versatile and most people find it easy to work with. With the introduction of the new editor Gutenberg it has become even more powerful and if you combine it with a custom front-end in react the possibilities are endless. This makes WP a so-called headless CMS which makes the content available via an API instead of doing it the WordPress way. There a lot of advantages of doing it the new this way, but sometimes doing it the old-fashioned way is the best approach. Want to know why or what it can do for you? Give me a call.

Technical SEO

You can promote your website as much as you want, link-build till you see blue in the face, but if your website is not optimized you're not getting anywhere. Technical Search Engine Optimization is the art of tweaking your website - in terms of indexability, speed and structure - so that search engines can index your website and your potential customers can find your website.

Webdesign

Modern website design can be subdivided in three equal parts. I can help you with all three so if you don't want to know the difference you don't have to. It can be subdivided in User Experience (UX) Design, User Interface (UI) design and Visual Design. When you hear webdesign most people think about visual design or graphic design. This is the kind that involves colors, fonts and making things look pretty. While I do think this is important, I feel UX and UI design are equally important (and often overlooked!) This is why an experienced web-designer will talk about your business goals before they talk about colors / fonts.
